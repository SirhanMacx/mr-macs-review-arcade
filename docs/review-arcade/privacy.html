Production publication status

This page is the Review Arcade privacy-policy candidate for App Store preparation. It is ready to publish as static policy content, but public release still requires live URL verification, final App Store privacy answers, and legal/privacy review before monetization.

Local-first profiles

Review Arcade stores player profiles, shards, cosmetic locker items, settings, local scores, and local progress on the device by default.

Optional leaderboards

If a global leaderboard endpoint is configured, score submissions may include a filtered public handle, anonymous client id, selected course, selected game or Review Board, score, timestamp, and trust metadata used for abuse prevention. Obvious personal information is scrubbed before storage.

Ads, purchases, and loot

The prototype bundles no ad SDK, purchase SDK, remote analytics SDK, or third-party tracking SDK. Cosmetic crates use earned gameplay shards only. Paid random loot is not enabled. Planned purchases are fixed, non-random products and remain disabled until StoreKit, receipt validation, entitlement restore, and legal/privacy review are complete.

Classroom and child-directed review

Review Arcade is school-themed and may be used by students. Before public release or monetization, the app requires COPPA, FERPA-adjacent, state student-privacy, leaderboard, ad, and purchase review.

Data controls

The app includes local export and reset controls in Settings. Local profile, shard, locker, room, and ranking data can be removed from the device by using the in-app reset controls.

For leaderboard removal or privacy questions, open a request at Review Arcade Support. Include the public handle, game or Review Board, approximate date, and score. Do not include student names, school ids, passwords, phone numbers, or private classroom records.
